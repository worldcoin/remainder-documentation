# Multilinear Extensions (MLEs)

Let $f(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n)$ be a function with coefficients $a_i \in \mathbb{F}$, such that inputs $x_j \in \{0, 1\}$, and outputs are $\in \mathbb{F}.$ Its multilinear extension $\widetilde{f}(x_1, \dots, x_n)$ is defined with inputs $x_j \in \mathbb{F}$, such that $\widetilde{f}(x) = f(x) \quad\!\!\!\forall\quad\!\!\!\!\! x \in \{0, 1\}^n$.
